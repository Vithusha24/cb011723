Introduction of wild life department


The Wildlife Department of Sri Lanka (DWC) is in charge of managing and conserving wild animals and their habitats, as well as being the guardian of the amazing natural heritage of the nation. The DWC, which was founded in 1949, is essential to preserving Sri Lanka's amazing biodiversity.
